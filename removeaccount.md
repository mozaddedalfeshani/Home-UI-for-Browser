## üìß How to Request Deletion

### Step 1: Compose an Email

Send an email to our developer at:

**Email Address:** `imurad2020@gmail.com`

### Step 2: Use the Subject Line

**Subject:** `Vintage World - Account Deletion Request`

### Step 3: Include Required Information

In your email, please include:

1. **Your registered email address** (the one you use to log into the app)
2. **Your full name** (as it appears in your account)
3. **Reason for deletion** (optional, but helps us improve)
4. **Confirmation statement:** "I confirm that I want to permanently delete my Vintage World account and all associated data."
